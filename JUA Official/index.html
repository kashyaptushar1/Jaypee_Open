Build career with aypee University Anoopshahr

Jaypee University Anoopshahr (JUA) is a multidisciplinary university established by the Govt. of U.P. Act No 8 of 2014. Based on the sanction received vide Govt. of Uttar Pradesh letter No 347/Sattar-1-2014-20(4)/2011 the University started functioning in 2014.

JUA is a New Generation multidisciplinary university offering futuristic programs and curricula as per vision and mission of the University for producing quality professionals and intellectuals capable of meeting global challenges. The University is set up in about 80 Acres of lush green environment on the banks of holy river Ganges, providing serenity and intellectually stimulating environment. Academic excellence, professionalism, leadership and team work are the hallmark of this university. We strive to uphold the highest standards in academics.

Students have remote access to supercomputing facility installed by the Jaypee Education system at the Ramanujam Universe, Noida.

Jaypee University Campus Highlights

A sprawling 95-acre campus bathed in the serenity of the Ganges River. That’s Jaypee University Anoopshahr! Imagine your campus tour – peeking into cutting-edge labs, getting lost in a maze of books in the library, and checking out the comfy dorms and happening cafes. But Jaypee goes beyond academics. You’ll be surrounded by a supportive alumni network, have the chance to make a difference through community projects, and even explore your passions with clubs and sports teams.

Jaypee offers top-notch education at a price that won’t break the bank. It’s like they say, affordable excellence – that’s the Jaypee way! Are you ready to write your own success story at Jaypee University Anoopshahr?
